Activities

  • Aerial Silks

    Have your ever experienced trapeze or aerial silks? Sail through the air and into a bed of foam as your become a circus extraordinaire!

  • Angled Trampolines

    Jumping up and down isn't always enough to DEFY limits.

  • Battle Beams

    If the balance beam is too low-key for you, amp it up with a little competition. Just grab a pugil stick, and battle it out gladiator style - last one standing wins!

  • Extreme Dodgeball

    What happens when you add dodgeballs, wall-to-wall trampolines and a cage together? Get ready for the most extreme game of your life!

  • Foam Pits

    Launch yourself through the air and into our MASSIVE FOAM PITS! Perform tricks on our special “Waterfall Trampolines” and experience extreme landing at its finest!

  • Ninja Course

    “Bow to your sensei” will have a whole new meaning! Now is YOUR SHOT to see if you have what it takes to conquer the course! Ninjas prepare for war!

  • Wall Tramp

    Bounce off the walls, literally. Become a parkour master with our wall tramps!
